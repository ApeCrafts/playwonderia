Team Wishlist on Steam Press Kit
Explore 'Wonderia', a unique open-world game set on an alien planet. Your journey combines survival, creativity, and exploration in a dynamic, procedurally generated environment.
Features
Vast Exploration
Discover a world alive with mystery, teeming with unique landscapes and secrets waiting to be uncovered.
Survival Combat
Engage in thrilling battles against alien creatures, employing strategy and a diverse arsenal to survive the unknown.
Sandbox Creativity
Shape the alien landscape with limitless construction possibilities, creating everything from shelters to entire cities.
Transformative Abilities
Harness unique powers to tackle challenging dungeons, with each transformation offering essential skills for navigation and combat.
